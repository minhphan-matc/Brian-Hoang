Esports

Esports is not your typical kind of sport. People might ask what kind of games make up esports but there are many. The top five Esports that had the largest prize money in 2017 are as follows:

  1. Dota 2           $38 Million
  2. Counter Strike: Global Offensive           $19 Million
  3. League of Legends           $12 Million
  4. Heroes of the Storm           $4.7 Million
  5. Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare           $4 Million

Forget Super Bowl rings and shiny gold medals. Today’s eSports champions are scoring massive million-dollar prize pools as spectators from around the world watch them game.
Esports are a rapidly growing community that is catching up to traditional sports in terms of viewership. The community that has interest from around the world is becoming the next big thing. It is certainly popular among the youth and without a doubt is making a huge impact on how we see video games today.
